24Hr Icecream shops and why they are great!!!!

Do you scream for ice cream? Is it your favourite guilty pleasure, even after a night out? For those of you that live in Daejeon, you need to visit Dalsi, the 24-hour self-service ice cream shop! It is located in Yuseong-gu, near Jangdae Middle and Elementary school. So, whether it is 3 in the afternoon or 3 in the morning, you can get your frozen treat fix here! Dalsi The store is open 24/7. You can come here anytime you want. What’s more there is a huge array of ice cream to choose from, including ice cream cones, ice pops, ice cream sandwiches — even ice cream cake! But what about the flavours? You’re practically guaranteed to find something in your taste. What’s your favourite? Chocolate, cookies and cream, mint choc chip, strawberry, watermelon, coffee, caramel etc.
